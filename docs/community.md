# Community

The docs cover the common questions, but some things are easier to figure out by asking someone who runs the same setup as you. That's what the community is for.

## Discord

Most of the community lives on [Discord](https://discord.autobrr.com/), with over 5,500 members. It's the fastest way to get help with your setup, and a good place to hang out even when nothing is broken: filter tips, tracker talk, seedbox and automation chat, and plenty of people who care about the same niche things you do. The developers are active there too.

[Join the Discord](https://discord.autobrr.com/)

## IRC

For a project built around IRC, it would be strange not to have a channel of our own. We run our own IRC server:

|             |                   |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Server**  | `irc.autobrr.com` |
| **Port**    | `6697` (TLS)      |
| **Channel** | `#chat`           |

Point your favorite IRC client at it and say hi.
